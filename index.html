About the Project

The Problem

Speaking is the universal measure for whether you "know" a language, but it's also one of the hardest skills to practice. Learners need consistent conversational partners and the confidence to speak even though they might sound "incorrect." We need to bridge this gap.

üë•

Our Users

Language learners who want to sound conversationally natural but lack regular opportunity to speak or the confidence to do so ‚Äî especially early-to-intermediate speakers who value naturalness over perfection.

üí°

Our Solution

An app where you conversationally speak with AI, and it tells you whether you sound conversationally natural, offering pointed improvements and specific study tips targeted towards talking in real-world scenarios.
